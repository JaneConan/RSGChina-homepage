Scrum Alliance （Scrum联盟）成立于 2001 年，是敏捷社区中规模最大、历史最悠久、最具影响力的专业会员和认证组织。Scrum Alliance 是一家非营利性协会，已为全球超过 100 万名从业者认证。 其愿景是帮助“改变工作的世界”，其使命是通过实践、原则和价值观指导和激励个人、领导者和组织，创造快乐、繁荣和可持续的工作场所。请访问 scrumalliance.org了解更多。

Founded in 2001, Scrum Alliance® is the largest, most established and influential professional membership and certification organization in the Agile community. Scrum Alliance is a nonprofit association that has certified more than 1,000,000 practitioners worldwide. Its vision is to help “Transform the World of Work” with a mission to guide and inspire individuals, leaders, and organizations with practices, principles, and values that create workplaces that are joyful, prosperous, and sustainable. Please visit scrumalliance.org.
